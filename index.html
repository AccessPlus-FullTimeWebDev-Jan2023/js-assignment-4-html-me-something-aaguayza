What is GUNPLA?

Gundams are japanese cartoon giant fighting robots that go into space and commit war crimes. Gundam Plastic, or Gunpla, is a hobby in which you can build these same robot in plastic form.

This giant robots are scaled down to 1/144, 1/100, or 1/60 depending on the type of model you choose to build.

What are all these gundam grade levels about??

Gunpla models are seprated to 4 primary grades

  • High Grade(1/144 scale)
  • Real Grade(1/144 scale
  • Master Grade (1/100 scale)
  • Perfect Grade(1/60 scale)
  • There are other grades and scales but for now we will focus on the 3 above

HG - High Grade(1/144 scale)

High Grade is the most commonly made model grade.

This grade is most suited for beginners as it is easy to build, more adffordable ($12-25$), and are small so you can collect a bunch!

Tony's recommended kits

  • HGUC RX-78-2 Gundam Revive($10<)
  • HG Gundam Barbatos ($14<)

MG - Master Grade(1/100 scale)

Master Grades are larger and more detailed than High Grade.

The process to build may take a little longer but the size, detail, and posability of these kits make the whole journey worth it!

Tony's recommended kits

  • MG Wing Zero Custom Ver Ka($45<)
  • MG MVF-X08 Eclipse Gundam ($53<)

RG - Real Grade(1/144 scale)

These kits have the detail and complexity of master grades but in the size of a High Grade!

While the parts are smaller, this is still a fun model grade to work with! This is a great choice if you want to collect what can be seen as mini Master Grades

Tony's recommended kits

  • RG RX-93-2 Hi-Nu Gundam($60<)
  • RG MSN-02 Zeong ($66<)

PG - Perfect Grade(1/60 scale)

Perfect grades are HUGE compared to the other grades. They come with more parts, detal, and even feture LED lights to give that sense of realism!

While this is the most expensive of the grades ($200+), they are still a great experience for beginners. These kits are also perfect for a group build since there are so many parts!

Tony's recommended kits

  • PG RX 0 Unicorn Gundam ($250<)
  • PG RX 78 Perfect Grade Unleashed ($300<)
