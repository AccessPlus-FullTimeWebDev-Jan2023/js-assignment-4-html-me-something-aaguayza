What is GUNPLA?

Gundams japanese cartoon giant fighting robots that go into space and commit war crimes. Gundam Plastic, or Gunpla, is a hobby in which you can build these same robot in plastic form.

This giant robots are scaled down to 1/144, 1/100, or 1/60 depending on the type of model you choose to build.

What are all these gundam grade levels about??

Gunpla models are seprated to 4 primary grades

  • High Grade(1/144 scale)
  • Real Grade(1/144 scale
  • Master Grade (1/100 scale)
  • Perfect Grade(1/60 scale)
  • There are other grades and scales but for now we will focus on the 3 above
